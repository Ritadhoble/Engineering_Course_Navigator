About RTMNU University

Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU), formerly known as Nagpur University, is a public state university located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. The Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University is one of the premier educational institutions of central India. It was established on August 4, 1923.

RTMNU enjoys connectivity with every part of the nation, making it a preferred choice for students from other states. The university offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in disciplines such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Management, and more.

RTMNU is committed to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation in various fields. It aims to empower students with knowledge and skills to contribute to society and the nation at large.
